Chess Piece Summary

The pieces and their allowed moves:

  • King: The king may move one square in any direction.
  • Queen: The queen can move any number of squares in any one direction.
  • Rook: Rooks may move any number of squares forwards, backwards, or side to side.
  • Bishop: Bishops may move any number of squares diagonally.
  • Knight: Knights may move two squares forward or backward in combination with one square sideways, OR two squares sideways and one square forwards or backwards.
  • Pawn: The pawns may move 2 squares forward on it's first move, or one square forwards otherwise. Can capture on direct adjacent diagonals.
